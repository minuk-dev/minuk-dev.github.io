종류

  • RAID 0 : stripping
  • RAID 1 : mirroring
  • RAID 5 : stripping + mirroring + parity (at least 3 disks)
  • RAID 6 : stripping + mirroring + parity (at least 4 disks)
  • RAID 0 + 1 : mirroring after stripping
  • RAID 10 (1 + 0) : stripping after mirroring
